3Q

An online building material and supplier library and comparator for designers, professional or recreational alike.

Its name comes from the SOP companies have for deciding on a specific supplier; to obtain at least 3 quotations from 3 different suppliers for comparison.

Hence the name, 3Q. It is read as ‘san Q’, a homophone for ‘thank you’.

Initial Observations

The problem:

Time-consuming and difficult for designers to source for building materials

Currently, architectural designers rely on internet research, recommendations from friends or colleagues, company libraries and/or supplier presentations to gather info on the material for use in a building project. In a building, there are numerous different types of materials used for many different spaces; each type may be supplied by different suppliers. As such, designers will have to comb through various sources in order to find the best match, and this is highly time-consuming and inefficient.

Hence, this led me to think about how we might assist designers in easily and efficiently obtaining info on materials and suppliers available in Singapore through a one-stop platform online.
